About Eldiron

Create your own classic role playing games (RPGs) with Eldiron, a cross platform and open source RPG Creator written in Rust.

Eldiron is currently under development and not yet ready for public consumption. You can follow the development progress on the blog.

Join the community on Discord or support the Eldiron project on Patreon.
